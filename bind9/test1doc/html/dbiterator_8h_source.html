82 /*%
83  * This structure is actually just the common prefix of a DNS db
84  * implementation's version of a dns_dbiterator_t.
85  *
86  * Clients may use the 'db' field of this structure. Except for that field,
87  * direct use of this structure by clients is forbidden. DB implementations
88  * may change the structure. 'magic' must be DNS_DBITERATOR_MAGIC for any of
89  * the dns_dbiterator routines to work. DB iterator implementations must

238  * database locks to be acquired. Rather than reacquire these locks every
239  * time one of these routines is called, the locks may simply be held.
